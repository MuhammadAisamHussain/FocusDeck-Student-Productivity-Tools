Our Services

🚢

Ocean Freight

ECARGOWORLD offers comprehensive Ocean Freight services from LCL to FCL, Reefer containers, RO-RO, and Break Bulk.

Whether you're shipping cars, heavy machinery, or commercial goods, our experts ensure your cargo reaches its destination on time and in the most cost-effective and secure manner possible.

  • LCL & FCL shipments
  • 20', 40', and reefer container handling
  • RO-RO shipping for vehicles and equipment
  • Break bulk and out-of-gauge cargo handling
  • Port-to-port and door-to-door solutions
  • End-to-end tracking and documentation support
✈️

Air Freight

At ECARGOWORLD Pakistan, we offer highly specialized Air Freight (Import & Export) solutions, managed by a team of logistics professionals with over 15 years of industry experience.

Thanks to our strong relationships with major international airlines, we provide our clients with priority space, competitive rates, and flexible transit options.

  • Priority and standard air cargo solutions
  • DDP/DDU shipment handling
  • Time-critical and express air freight
  • Dangerous goods transportation (IATA regulations)
  • Consolidation and deconsolidation services
  • Real-time tracking and shipment visibility
